Logo Design Concepts (Set 2)

Focus: Interlocking "EC" / Rubik's Cube Style

Option 1: Isometric Cube

E and C mapped onto the faces of a 3D cube. Structural and precise.

Option 2: Puzzle Blocks

Constructed from individual "pixels" or blocks, interlocking to form the initials.

Option 3: The Interlock

Two geometric shapes overlapping and blending (screen mode) to create a unified form.

Option 4: The Grid

A literal 3x3 grid where the lit squares form the letters. Very reminiscent of a Rubik's face.

Option 5: The Folded Box

A 3D perspective of a box unfolding. The left side forms E, the right outline forms C.
